Our Mission

The Human Temple is a non-profit association dedicated to fostering awareness through the body. We believe the human form is a sacred vessel—an ever-changing temple that reflects the rhythms of life. Through movement, breath, and stillness, we return to presence and reclaim our inner wisdom.

Our Offerings

Yoga Classes: Traditional Ashtanga Mysore style and guided sessions for all levels.

Workshops & Retreats: Immersive experiences in nature combining movement, meditation, and community.

1-on-1 Guidance: Personalized practices tailored to your physical, emotional, and energetic state.

Community Gatherings: Ceremonies, talks, and shared spaces to reconnect with self and others.

Meet Lucía

Lucía Echenique is a devoted yoga practitioner, teacher, and seeker of presence. Rooted in the Ashtanga tradition, she guides others from a place of embodied wisdom and compassionate awareness.

After leaving behind a life that no longer aligned with her values, she chose the path of simplicity, nature, and inner work. She believes that movement, breath, and silence are gateways to remembering who we truly are.

Lucía created The Human Temple to honor the body as a sacred vessel, and to offer others a space to reconnect—with themselves, with stillness, and with community.
